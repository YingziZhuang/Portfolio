Ouah Ouah Friends - User Research

The recent improvement in technology helps pet owners easily accesses the various pet community. They can join the pet owner communities by searching for them on social media. However, some pet owners are still unwilling to join these communities because of several unknown reasons. This makes the pet owners hard to gain long-term support for their pets. In this paper, we conduct quantitative research to explore the pet ownersâ€™ main points for joining the community.

Several Human Computer Interaction(HCI) methods: Photo Elicitation, Mapping, Digital Ethnography and Observation were used to identify the pain points for pet owners' community and purposed new product.
